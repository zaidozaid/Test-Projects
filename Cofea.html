Welcome to SheeZu's CofeeShop

Coffee Shop is your go-to neighborhood joint, any time, any day. Join us for specialty coffee to pep you up in the morning, organic smoothies, juices, and food to fuel your day

The Coffee Shop opened in July 2017 and is owned by young entrepreneur Sheezan Yousuf . We feature all homemade recipes and fresh ingredients that we are certain you and your family will enjoy. Our strength lies in our coffee drinks. Fresh brewed every day, we have dozens of combinations of espresso beverages served hot or cold.

Come sit on our patio and enjoy a weekend morning reading the paper, spending time with your friends and family, relaxing with your laptop on our free wi-fi, or make some new friends from the neighborhood at our big farm table.
